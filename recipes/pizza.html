Pizza

Description

Pizza, originating from Italy is a classic food that we all love. New york style pizza, chicago deep dish, or thin crust, you can not go wrong with any kind of pizza. A diverse food that can have almost any toppings you can think of! Pinapples? Olives? Ham? Tomatoes? Almost anything can be a topping on pizza, allowing it to be one of the most diverse foods you can make.
